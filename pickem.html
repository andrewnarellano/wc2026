How to Play

  1. 1 Enter the score you think each team will finish with using the boxes on either side of the matchup.
  2. 2 If you predict a tied score, penalty kick fields will appear automatically — enter how many PKs you think each team scores to pick the winner.
  3. 3 Hit Submit Pick, then enter your first name and last initial. Your pick is saved below the match for everyone to see.
  4. 4 The team with the higher score is your predicted winner. If you called a draw, the PK winner counts instead.

Picks are saved locally in your browser. Everyone entering picks on the same device will see each other's entries in the table.
