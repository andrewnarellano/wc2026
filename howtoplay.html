How to Play Pick'em

Make your score predictions for every match and see how your picks stack up against everyone else on the same device.

1
Enter your score prediction
Use the boxes on either side of the matchup to enter the score you think each team will finish with. Scores can be 0–99.
2
Tied score? Pick the PK winner
If you predict both teams finish with the same score, a Penalty Kicks row will appear automatically. Enter how many PKs you think each team converts to decide your winner.
3
Submit your pick
Tap Submit Pick and enter your first name and last initial. That's all — your prediction is locked in immediately.
4
See how everyone did
Your pick is logged in the table below the match, showing your name, the team you predicted to win, and the exact score you called. The team with the higher score is your winner — if you predicted a draw, the PK winner counts instead.
